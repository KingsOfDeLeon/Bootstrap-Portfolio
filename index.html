About Me

VizExplorer is a software company focusing on improving operational intelligence to maximize client revenue streams. Currently we're making huge strides in improving the casino gaming industry through the use of proprietary data visualization software as well as industry expertise. I specifically am responsible for deployments and upgrades of VizExplorer software with a heavy influence on the progression of new technology and products.

If you have a chance, check out my portfolio!
